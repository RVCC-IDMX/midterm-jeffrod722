Ellescaped Boutique

About Ellescaped

Hello! My name is Emily Lescano and I love all things fashion. I was the president of a club during my college years called RUThrifty, where the goal of the club is to show and tell people about the ways and benefits of upcycling your clothing and showing new ways of altering and working with the pieces you already have

Portfolio

Project 1 : Denim Dress

This dress is made up of old denim jeans my father no longer needed. I decided to test my skills and make my own dress out of them.

Project 2 : Mushroom Girl Costume

The mushroom outfit was made from scratch with leftover fabric from high-end fashion boutiques in New York City that were saved from the landfill and resold, plus cardboard and other 'trash' household items that otherwise would have been thrown out into a landfill

Project 3 : Old Shirt Blanket

This blanket is made from all my old school t-shirts that I no longer wanted to wear or didn't fit but I also couldn't really donate
